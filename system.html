Integrated System Diagram

Above is the final system diagram of Terrapin. An Arduino collects data from multiple sensors and informs a Raspberry Pi, the brain of our system. Based on these readings, an algorithm decides the best direction for the turtle to wander. This 'best' direction is determined based on the light and temperature requirements of the plant contained in the shell. Once decided, the choice is returned to the Arduino, which actuates motors connected to linkage-based legs. The turtle thus wanders about, taking the best care of its plant that it can.
